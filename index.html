Who we are

At Jade Bar, we're passionate about creating unique, memorable experiences through our craft cocktails and exceptional service. What started as a vision between friends has grown into a fully mobile bar service that caters to events of all kinds. From chic weddings to high-energy parties, we bring creativity, fun, and professionalism wherever we go. We believe every celebration deserves more than just good drinksâ€”it deserves an experience. That's why we work closely with our clients to design a custom bar experience that matches the theme and style of your event.

With years of bartending expertise, we know how to mix up delicious drinks while keeping the energy of your event flowing. Our goal is simple: to ensure your guests are raving about your event long after the last drink has been poured.
